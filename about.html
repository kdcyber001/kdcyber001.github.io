Kofi Danso
KD Cyber Solutions

KD Cyber Solutions is a sole-proprietor federal IT and cybersecurity firm based in Germantown, Maryland. Founded by Kofi Danso, the company draws on enterprise-level experience and a rigorous certification background to deliver dependable security and IT solutions to government contractors and small businesses.

Prior to founding KD Cyber Solutions, Kofi served as an IT Team Lead and Lead Implementation Engineer at Capgemini, where he supported large-scale enterprise IT deployments. He maintains an active portfolio of industry certifications.
